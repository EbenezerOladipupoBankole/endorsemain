# Endorse App - Demo Walkthrough Script

## **Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)**
*Visual Cue: Face camera or show Endorse Landing Page logo.*

"Hi everyone. Today I’m excited to show you **Endorse**—the fastest way to sign and manage your documents. Whether you're dealing with a standard PDF or a Word doc, Endorse handles the conversion, signing, and security so you can get back to business."

---

## **Step 1: The Dashboard (0:30 - 1:00)**
*Visual Cue: Show mouse hovering over the stats cards.*

"Here we are on the **Dashboard**. As you can see, it provides a clean overview of my activity.
- On the left, I can quickly check my usage limits and plan status.
- In the center, I have my quick stats: Total Documents, Signed, and Pending.
- And below, I have a list of my **Recent Documents** so I can quickly pick up where I left off."

---

## **Step 2: Uploading a Document (1:00 - 1:45)**
*Visual Cue: Open a folder window next to the browser to drag-and-drop.*

"Let's start a new signing session. While I can upload a PDF directly, Endorse is smart—it also supports **Microsoft Word documents**.

I’m going to drag in this contract, which is a `.docx` file. You’ll notice the system instantly recognizes it and converts it to a secure, signable PDF format automatically. No need for external tools."

---

## **Step 3: Signing the Document (1:45 - 2:30)**
*Visual Cue: Zoom in slightly on the document viewer when the file loads.*

"Now we are in the **Signing Interface**.
1.  First, I need a signature. I'll click **'Create Signature'** (or use the sidebar).
2.  I can type my name or draw it manually. Let's draw it right here on the screen. Looks good, I'll click **Save**.
3.  Now, I simply **click anywhere on the document** to place my signature.
4.  I can drag and resize it to fit perfectly on the signature line."

*Visual Cue: Place the signature, maybe resize it once to show flexibility.*

---

## **Step 4: Completion & Download (2:30 - 3:00)**
*Visual Cue: Click the button and open the downloaded PDF to show the final result.*

"Once the signature is placed, I’m done. I just click **'Download Signed PDF'**.
The system flattens the document, embeds my signature securely, and downloads the finalized, legally binding PDF to my device."

---

## **Step 5: Inviting Others (Optional - Pro Feature) (3:00+)**
*Visual Cue: Hover over the 'Send Invites' button but don't click if not demonstrating full flow.*

"If I needed someone else to sign this, I could use the **'Send Invites'** feature to email them a secure link to this document, allowing us to collaborate asynchronously."

---

## **Closing**
"And that is Endorse—simple, professional document signing for everyone. Thanks for watching!"
